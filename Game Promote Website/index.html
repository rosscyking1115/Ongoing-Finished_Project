Mechanics

These are the 4 types of mechanics of the game.

Mechanic 1: Cherry Counter

The counter increases with each cherry collected by the player.

Mechanic 2: Moving Platform

The platform is moving from 2 invisible cordinates, and
player will encounter both placed horizontally or vertically.

Mechanic 3: Spikes

The player will die upon contact with them, which are strategically
placed on walls and the ground throughout the game."

Mechanic 4: Saw

Collision with these saws will cause the player's character
to die. and there are many variation of them.
